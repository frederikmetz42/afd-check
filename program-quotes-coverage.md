## Notable gaps / asymmetries

- **Rente (2016):** The Grundsatzprogramm has no standalone old-age pension reform concept. Pension appears only narrowly (crediting child-rearing; reforming MP pension privileges). The full pension concept — Austria comparison, including civil servants/politicians, flexible retirement, 'Junior-Spardepot' — is a 2025-manifesto addition.
- **Pflege / Gesundheitssystem (2016):** Only home/family care is addressed (5.4.4). No hospital-financing or wider health-system concept. The afd-check statement ("Im Gesundheitssystem läuft alles schief") is best matched by the 2025 health chapter.
- **corruption vs. establishment:** Both topics draw on the same anti-cartel framing in the 2016 platform (S. 8). Quotes were split so corruption = Filz/Lobbyismus/term-limits, establishment = political class + taxes.

## Verbatim-accuracy notes (transcription caveats, all resolved)

- Two 2016 quotes (patriotism free-speech, gender Geschlechterpädagogik) run across a two-column page break; only the contiguous verbatim clause was used, with the natural continuation marked in brackets `[…]`.
- The 2016 Rente quote ("…im Beruf zurückstecken musste…") is a margin pull-quote on S. 37; the verb **zurückstecken** was re-checked against the PDF (an earlier draft typo "zurücksticken" was corrected).
- Umlauts/ß in `program-quotes.json` are ASCII-transliterated (ae/oe/ue/ss) for safe JSON merging; the page references point to the original spelling in the PDF.

Every quote in `program-quotes.json` is verbatim from the located official source PDF, with page and section recorded. No quote was invented, paraphrased-as-quote, or sourced second-hand.
